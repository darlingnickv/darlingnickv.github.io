    Nicholas Viau is a photographer based in the southeast US. He was born and raised in Southwest Ranches, Florida. Nicholas graduated from Florida State University with a Bachelors of Science in Psychology. After college, he moved to Atlanta where his photographic work began in earnest, citing early attempts dating back to high school. Nicholas specializes in concerned photography with a inclination towards environmental portraits.
